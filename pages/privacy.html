Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Our Core Privacy Promise

SecretNote is designed from the ground up so that we cannot read your notes — not just that we choose not to. Your note is encrypted in your browser using AES-256-GCM before anything is stored. The decryption key lives only in the URL fragment you share. Our servers never receive or store the key.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • Note content (it is encrypted and we have no key)
  • Your name, email, or any personal information
  • Account data (no accounts exist)
  • The content of URL fragments (browsers do not send these to servers)

3. What We Do Collect

Like all websites, our server may log standard access information: IP address (anonymised), browser type, page visited, and timestamp. This is used solely for security monitoring and aggregate traffic analysis.

4. Storage

Encrypted note data is stored in your browser's localStorage, not on our servers. Clearing your browser site data for this domain will delete any notes you have created that have not yet been read.

5. Advertising

SecretNote displays third-party advertisements (such as Google AdSense). These networks may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can opt out at aboutads.info/choices.

6. Cookies

We do not set any first-party cookies. Third-party advertising networks may set cookies for ad personalisation purposes.

7. Children

SecretNote is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to us via our website contact details.
